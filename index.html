About Us

Kalki Paltan Foundation is a dedicated non-profit organization committed to empowering communities through social, educational, and healthcare initiatives. We promote Hindu unity and support underprivileged groups by providing education, healthcare, and elder care services.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To create a socially inclusive and empowered society where everyone has access to education, healthcare, and community support.

Our Mission

To uplift the underprivileged through sustainable education, elderly care, and healthcare solutions while preserving Hindu values and unity.

Our Solutions

  • Education: Scholarships, school infrastructure, mentorship programs.
  • Senior Care: Elder care programs, old-age homes, community support.
  • Healthcare: Plans to develop a hospital and mobile clinics for affordable care.
  • Unity & Culture: Awareness programs and cultural events promoting Hindu unity.

Fund Utilization

  • 35%: Education Programs
  • 30%: Healthcare Initiatives
  • 25%: Elderly Care Support
  • 10%: Community Outreach & Awareness
